Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Shiori EPUB Reader ("the App") is developed by Endofday. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • The App does not require you to create an account to read books.
  • The developer does not operate any server and does not receive, store, or have access to your books, reading data, or personal information.
  • The App does not collect your name or any personally identifiable information for the developer's own use.
  • By default, EPUB files you open are read locally on your device and are never transmitted. They leave your device only if you choose to enable the optional Cloud Sync feature described below, in which case they are synced with your own Google Drive (specifically under the "Shiori Library" folder or the private application folder).

Information Collected by Third-Party Services

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may automatically collect certain device information to serve personalised or non-personalised ads, including:

  • Advertising ID (Android Ad ID)
  • Device model, OS version, and language
  • IP address (approximate location)
  • App usage events (e.g., ad impressions, clicks)

For details on how Google collects and processes this data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob's data collection practices.

2. Google Drive Cloud Sync (Optional)

The App includes an optional Cloud Sync feature that lets you back up your data to, and restore it from, your own Google Drive. This feature is off by default and is only used if you choose to sign in with Google from the Settings screen. If you never sign in, no data is ever sent to Google Drive and none of the following applies.

Google account access we request

  • Your email address (email scope) — shown in the app only so you can see which account is signed in. It is not stored or sent anywhere else.
  • Google Drive access (https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive scope) — this allows the App to read, list, and write files in your Google Drive. We use this scope specifically to locate or create a folder named "Shiori Library" where you can upload your EPUB books, list the files inside it, and download them to your device. The App also backs up settings and reading progress (bookmarks and notes) to its private folder. No other files in your Google Drive are accessed or modified.

What is stored in your Drive (only when you enable Cloud Sync)

  • App settings (font size, line height, theme, text-to-speech voice/speed/language, and your text-replacement rules)
  • Reading data: per-book reading position, bookmarks, highlights, and notes
  • For books you explicitly choose to sync: the EPUB file and its cover image

How this data is handled

  • All of this data is stored directly in your own Google Drive (under the "Shiori Library" folder and the hidden app folder). It is transferred straight between your device and your Google account.
  • The developer has no server and never receives, stores, accesses, or processes this data. No human reads it.
  • The data is used solely to provide the sync/backup feature you requested. It is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising or any other purpose.

Removing your data and revoking access

  • In the App you can sign out at any time, and remove individual synced books from Drive from the Cloud Sync screen.
  • You can revoke the App's access to your Google account at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions. Removing the App from your Google account also deletes its hidden app-data folder and everything in it.

Limited Use disclosure. Shiori EPUB Reader's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

3. In-App Purchases (Google Play Billing)

The App may offer digital content, features, or services for purchase within the App (In-App Purchases). All payments and digital purchases are processed, managed, and secured exclusively via the Google Play Billing Library.

  • No Financial Data Collection: The developer does not collect, store, access, or process any payment details, credit card numbers, billing addresses, or bank account credentials. All financial transactions are handled directly and securely by Google Play.
  • Purchase Validation: To unlock and maintain access to premium features you purchase, the App utilizes secure transaction tokens provided by the Google Play Billing API. This purchase history data is stored locally on your device and is only transmitted directly to Google's verification servers to confirm purchase validity.
  • Refund and Transaction Policies: All purchases are subject to Google Play's Terms of Service and refund policies. You can manage your purchases and subscriptions through your Google Play Account settings.

4. Permissions Used

Permission Purpose
INTERNET Required to load Google AdMob advertisements and, if you enable Cloud Sync, to communicate with Google Drive
FOREGROUND_SERVICE
FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK		 Keeps Text-to-Speech reading active when the screen is off or another app is in the foreground
SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW Displays the floating TTS controls overlay so you can pause/skip without returning to the App
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Shows a TTS playback notification in the status bar while reading is active
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Allows opening EPUB files from your Downloads folder on Android 12 and below only

5. Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device (and, only if you enable Cloud Sync, a copy in your own Google Drive as described in Section 2):

  • Book library (file paths, titles, authors, cover images)
  • Reading progress per book
  • Text replacement rules you create
  • App settings (font size, TTS speed, theme, etc.)
  • Bookmarks, highlights, and notes

This data is never transmitted to any developer-operated server. It is sent only to your own Google Drive, and only if you enable the optional Cloud Sync feature.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The advertisements displayed by AdMob are subject to Google's families policy.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
