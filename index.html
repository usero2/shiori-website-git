Deep Dive Features

Deep Dive into Pro Features

Features built to give you ultimate control over your reading experience.

🔊

Advanced TTS & Language Control

Precise playback control with smart multi-language automatic voice switching.

  • 🎯 Auto-detect Script: Auto-detects script of 6 languages (TH, EN, ZH, JA, KO, DE) and switches voices seamlessly per paragraph.
  • ▲▼ Priority Ordering: Resolve shared script conflicts (Latin for EN/DE, Han for ZH/JA) by dragging script priorities.
  • ⇅ Auto-Scroll & Highlight: Auto-scrolls reader view to keep up with the currently read paragraph.
✏️

Dual-Mode Text Replacement

Fix pronunciation issues or visual layouts independently with advanced rule configuration.

  • 🎨 Visual Replacement: Replace matching words visually in the reader and for TTS reading alike.
  • 🔊 TTS Replacement: Replace words only when sent to TTS engine, leaving original text visible on the page.
  • ⚡ Import/Export System: Fast rule searching, Regex support, and import/export via tab-separated text files.
📱

Smart Floating Panel

Keep listening in the background or when screen is off, with interactive floating controls.

  • 🤏 Pinch to Resize: Adjust the text box viewport without scaling the reading font size.
  • 🧲 Snap to Edge: Drags >50% off-screen snap to 15% visibility, keeping screen clear for other apps.
  • 🔄 Double-Tap to Center: Quickly double-tap the panel to animate it back to center of the screen.
⚙️

Advanced Visual Customization

Overcome rendering issues and custom styles embedded in book files.

  • 🔤 Force System Font: Force standard device font (like Noto Thai) for stable Thai text reading.
  • 🚫 Disable EPUB CSS: Discard publisher stylesheets and format the layout using the app's clean defaults.
  • 🌈 Remove Font Color: Strips hardcoded colors to maintain eye comfort in Dark or Sepia themes.
☁️

Google Drive Cloud Sync

Optional cloud sync. Save library books, reading progress, and text replacement lists directly to your private Google Drive. 100% secure: we have no database server and cannot access your data.

  • ✓ Direct secure connection between app and Google Drive
  • ✓ Sync across all your personal Android devices
  • ✓ Data stays inside your own storage control
